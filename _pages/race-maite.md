## The Racing Companion Built for Sailors

Race Maite keeps you focused on the water. Track your starts, monitor laylines, record splits, and review every race — all from your phone.

### Features

- **Start Timer** — Precision countdown with fleet sync and line bias indicator
- **Layline Tracker** — Real-time VMG and tack angle guidance
- **Race Log** — Automatic recording of course, splits, and finish time
- **Post-Race Review** — Replay your track with performance overlays
- **Fleet Mode** — Share position with teammates during training sessions

## Support

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Which devices are supported?**  
Race Maite runs on iOS 16+ and Android 10+.

**Does it work offline?**  
Yes. GPS tracking and race logging work fully offline. Fleet Mode requires an active connection.

**How do I export my race data?**  
Go to Race Log → select a race → tap Share → choose CSV or GPX format.

**How do I reset my fleet session?**  
Open Settings → Fleet Mode → End Session.
