## Full Fleet Visibility, Anywhere

Fleet Scope gives managers and coaches a live picture of every vessel in their fleet — position, speed, heading, and status — in a single dashboard.

### Features

- **Live Map** — Real-time AIS and GPS overlay for all registered vessels
- **Vessel Profiles** — Detailed records including specs, maintenance history, and crew
- **Alerts** — Geofence, speed, and offline notifications per vessel
- **Reporting** — Exportable trip logs, utilization reports, and incident summaries
- **Multi-fleet Support** — Manage multiple fleets under one account with role-based access

## Support

### Frequently Asked Questions

**How many vessels can I track?**  
The free tier supports up to 5 vessels. Pro and Enterprise plans have no limit.

**What positioning systems are supported?**  
Fleet Scope ingests AIS Class A/B, NMEA 0183, and direct GPS via the companion app.

**Can I set up geofence alerts?**  
Yes. Open a vessel profile → Alerts → Add Geofence and draw your boundary on the map.

**Is my data stored securely?**  
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. See our [Privacy Policy](#) for details.

**How do I add a new vessel?**  
Go to Fleet → Add Vessel, enter the MMSI or pair via the onboard GPS module.
